Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Smart technology, green energy, digital economy, inclusive growth, sustainable future.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly policies, renewable energy, resilient infrastructure, green economy, inclusive development.

Infrastructure

Basic roads, limited connectivity, manual operations, insufficient facilities, developing transport systems.

Empowerment

Limited education, gender inequality, minimal resources, rural focus, emerging awareness initiatives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Our journey towards a new Bangladesh is not about instant results, but about starting with a strong foundation of principles and pragmatism. We must prioritize meaningful reforms over superficial actions, avoiding arbitrary decisions. Citizens aren't looking for quick fixes; they want a sustainable, principled approach that respects the complexity of the task."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
